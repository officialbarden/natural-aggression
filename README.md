# Natural Aggression
This datapack adds Beef between mobs.
Mobs will fight other mobs or be targetted by mobs.

the Undead Zombies: [Zombies, Husks & Drowned]
They have beef among each other.

the Nether Foes: [Piglins, Piglin Brutes, Hoglins]
They collectively have beef with Every Other Sub Category of Mobs.

the Skeletons: [Skeleton, Wither Skeleton, Bogged, Strays]
They do not have beef with the Undead Zombies, but they have beef with other mobs.

the Endermen: [Enderman, Ender Dragon]
Endermen are neutral and do not start beef, but they fight back almost immediately.

[Wolves] have beef with [cats, fox, rabbits, sheeps, cows, parrots, goats, armadillos, bats, pigs, chickens] as they see these animals as food.
